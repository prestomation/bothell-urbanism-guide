        Urbanism Glossary#

        Plain-language definitions of terms you’ll hear at community meetings, in city council sessions, and in urbanist media. If you’ve ever nodded along while someone talked about “FAR” or “SEPA” and had no idea what they meant – this is for you.

        Terms are grouped by topic. Each entry tries to explain not just what a term means but why it matters in Bothell.

        How to use this glossary#

        Browse by category below, or use the site search to find a specific term. Each term page includes:

        • A plain-language definition
        • Why it matters for Bothell specifically
        • Related terms you might also want to know

        Categories#

        • Housing & Zoning – ADU, FAR, HB 1110, middle housing, and more
        • Transportation – BRT, mode share, complete streets, and more
        • Land Use & Planning – SEPA, comprehensive plan, GMA, and more
        • Funding & Policy – TIF, MFTE, impact fees, and more

        Don’t see a term? Open an issue or submit a PR.
